CSSConf is a one-day front-end and design-oriented conference for builders of the web. We are a part of JSFest Oakland. Everyone is welcome.

Buy a ticket The ticket covers entry to all JSFest events.

Cast

In alphabetical order.

Daniel Eden

Daniel Eden is a Designer at Dropbox, working mostly on web, interaction, and revenue. Before joining Dropbox, he wrote about web typography for Monotype/Typecast, and designed and built numerous web tools such as Animate.css and Onword, an online writing environment.

Outside of Dropbox, he has a passion for the web—in particular, web typography and animation—and continues to write on his blog about Design, courage, and lack thereof.

Elyse Holladay

Elyse is an instructor and designer/developer at MakerSquare, ATXSass co-organizer, and conference speaker. She’s spent her career writing HTML, CSS, and Sass for large product apps, and is always willing to talk about refactoring, women in tech, GIFs, makeup, yoga, and good wine.

Alex Sexton

Alex Sexton is a Product Engineer at Stripe living out of Austin, TX. He is on the jQuery Board and the Modernizr Team and takes himself extremely seriously.

Premiere

CSSConf will take place at The New Parkway Theater—an eclectic, picture pizza pub-inspired movie theater in Oakland, CA. It's coveniently located at 474 24th Street, right off of Telegraph Avenue.

Call for Papers

The Call for Papers for CSSConf is open. We're gathering the submissions on Github. If you are not feeling comfortable with applying publicly, please drop a line at contact+cfp@cssconfoak.land—we provide full discretion. Feel free to contact us with any questions. We are happy to help with writing a proposal too.
