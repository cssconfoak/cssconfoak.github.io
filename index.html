Venue

CSSConf will take place at The New Parkway Theater—an eclectic, picture pizza pub-inspired movie theater in Oakland, CA. It's conveniently located at 474 24th Street, right off of Telegraph Avenue.

The conference will start at 10am and run until 5pm with lunch and shorter breaks.

Sponsors

We're looking for amazing companies willing to support the event. Sponsorship information can be found on JSFest site. If you are interested in sponsoring, please drop an email to contact+sponsor@cssconfoak.land for more details.
