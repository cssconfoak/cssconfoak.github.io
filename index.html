CSSConf is a one-day front-end and design-oriented conference for builders of the web. Everyone is welcome.

Buy a ticket The ticket covers entry to all JSFest events.

Cast

In alphabetical order.

Michelle Bu

Michelle works on product at Stripe and maintains PeerJS from Oakland, CA.

Outside of writing code, she enjoys Diet Coke® and fried chicken, often at the same time.

Elyse Holladay

Elyse is an instructor and designer/developer at MakerSquare, ATXSass co-organizer, and conference speaker.

She’s spent her career writing HTML, CSS, and Sass for large product apps, and is always willing to talk about refactoring, women in tech, GIFs, makeup, yoga, and good wine.

Brenna O'Brien

Brenna is a front-end developer, creator of csshexagon.com, and lead instructor at HackerYou and Ladies Learning Code. She loves experimenting with new web technology and pushing the boundaries of what CSS and JavaScript can do, especially if that involves weird math calculations.

When she's not building websites, you'll find her cycling around Toronto and flooding the Internet with cat pictures.

Adam Morse

Adam Morse is a designer trying to make the web a nicer place for users.

Mark Otto

Mark is co-creator of Bootstrap, the world’s most popular front-end framework, and currently works at GitHub. He once designed Britney Spears’ website, plays a lot of Destiny, and is a huge nerd.

Claudina Sarahe

Claudina Sarahe is a NYC-based entrepreneur, front-end architect, organizer, and speaker. She organizes SassConf and runs the monthly Sass meetup.

When she's not working on her own projects you can find her with the Oddbirds; eating dessert with a soup spoon and molding ideas into beautiful, usable experiences. Keep up with her radical shenanigans @itsmisscs.

Alex Sexton

Alex Sexton is a Product Engineer at Stripe living out of Austin, TX. He is on the jQuery Board and the Modernizr Team and takes himself extremely seriously.

Connor Sears

Connor is co-creator of the mobile front-end framework, Ratchet. Currently, he's at GitHub helping create a better way to design, build and ship software. Originally from Kentucky, Connor has a special bond with all kinds of bourbon.

Kristina Schneider

Kristina Schneider leads Application Design at ReadyForZero and lives and travels between San Francisco and Berlin.

She organizes CSSconf EU, runs the monthly up.front meetup, and teaches front end development and web design in workshops and free community classes.

Ainsley Wagoner

Ainsley Wagoner is a Visual UX designer in San Francisco and 2014 Code for America Fellow. She spent her childhood putting skittles in rainbow order and has always believed pink & green is the ideal combo.

Ainsley loves shaping the visual direction of digital products as well as the details of its implementation. She is a fan of CSS, Illustrator, patterns, vector graphics, Beyoncé and Boston Terriers.

Jennifer Wong

Jennifer Wong is a self taught web developer. She spends her days on the front end, making the web a prettier and more functional place.

In a former life, she was a civil engineer and is still a registered Engineer in Training (No. EIT 121059) with the California Department of Consumer Affairs. She's a jane of all trades and loves eating, coding, and sleeping.

Venue

CSSConf will take place at The New Parkway Theater—an eclectic, picture pizza pub-inspired movie theater in Oakland, CA. It's conveniently located at 474 24th Street, right off of Telegraph Avenue.

The conference will start at 10am and run until 5pm with lunch and shorter breaks.
